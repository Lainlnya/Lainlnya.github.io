What is Mud?

Program Guide

Mud means muddy soil with water, and it is formed by the decomposition of microorganisms through geological and chemical actions over a long period of time by depositing clay substances containing mud, decomposition products of animals, plants, soil and salts.

Mud Efficacy

Mud generally contains a large amount of various active ingredients such as natural minerals that prevent skin aging.

Minerals that give vitality and elasticity to the skin. It is rich in skin and nourishing contains colloidal ingredients and it has a crystalline component, so it can be used in physical treatment methods.

Mud has antibacterial properties, so it is effective for trauma treatment.
